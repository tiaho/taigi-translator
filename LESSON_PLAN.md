## Curriculum Overview

This curriculum is designed to teach Taiwanese through practical, real-world scenarios with direct comparison to Taiwan Mandarin (åœ‹èª). Each unit builds on previous knowledge and emphasizes the unique vocabulary, grammar, and cultural aspects of Taiwanese.

### Learning Philosophy
- **Comparative Approach**: Every lesson explicitly compares Taiwanese to Taiwan Mandarin to highlight differences
- **Practical Focus**: Emphasize real-world scenarios and everyday conversations
- **Cultural Context**: Include cultural notes explaining when and why to use Taiwanese
- **Tone Awareness**: Build understanding of tone sandhi gradually through exposure
- **Audio-First**: Heavy emphasis on listening and pronunciation from day one

### Proficiency Levels
- **Beginner (Units 1-5)**: Basic survival Taiwanese for everyday situations
- **Intermediate (Units 6-10)**: Extended conversations and common social interactions
- **Advanced (Units 11-15)**: Complex topics, idiomatic expressions, and cultural nuance
